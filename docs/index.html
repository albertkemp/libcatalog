NOTE: THIS IS JUST OUR LITTLE PRIVATE LIBRARY, NOT A PUBLIC LIBRARY.

You can borrow, renew or return books here. You should also make use of the Catalog.

Notes:

None of the content in the books is allowed for copying, modifying or used to make profit. This is a not-for profit organisation to borrow books off, and you will have to come to the library's home. There you can get the books ordered at the Catalog.

Instructions for borrowing:

  1. Go to the Catalog
  2. Choose which books you want to borrow (up to 5)
  3. Click the borrow button which leads to the borrowing form.
  4. Fill out the form and click SUBMIT

You now have a book borrowed out of Fenton Library that you can go and pick up.


Renewing and Returning:

Go to the renew page for renewing and the return page for returning. There will be forms that you fill out and you can renew for another 5 days at most.

info  You have to drop the books off at the library when you have returned them - there is no returns chute.

Alternatively, instead of the QR-code you can ask for use of one of the Library monitors to borrow out your book.
